About Us

Developer & Designer

The goal of the N-Archive is to recommend a good and entertaining Webtoon that can take away your boredom, This website will not just recommend we will also show why the Webtoon that we recommend is nice and tell you the synopsis of the story and the name of the artist and also show the ratings in My Anime List so that we can ensure you that you can read a wonderful and exciting Webtoon.

Mision

To become the ultimate go-to hub for discovering captivating and high-quality Webtoons that spark joy, fight boredom, and connect readers to the best stories the medium has to offer.
