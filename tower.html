Tower

Description

The "Tower" genre in manhwa is a captivating subgenre that intertwines elements of fantasy, action, and psychological drama. Central to these narratives is the concept of ascending a mysterious, often colossal tower, where each floor presents unique challenges, adversaries, and revelations. This genre has garnered significant popularity, especially with the rise of digital platforms like Webtoon, introducing readers to intricate worlds filled with suspense and adventure.
