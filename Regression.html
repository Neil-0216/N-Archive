Regression

Description

The regression genre in manhwa has become increasingly popular for its gripping blend of fantasy, action, and second-chance storytelling. At its core, this genre features protagonists who are sent back in time—often to a critical moment in their past life—with full knowledge of future events. Armed with experience and foresight, they set out to right past wrongs, prevent tragedies, or pursue ultimate power, creating compelling narratives rich in strategy, revenge, and personal growth.
