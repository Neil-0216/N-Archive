Murim

Description

The Murim genre in webtoons offers a captivating blend of martial arts, fantasy, and philosophical themes, drawing readers into a world where strength, honor, and personal growth are paramount. Originating from Korean interpretations of Chinese wuxia traditions, Murim narratives have carved a unique niche in digital storytelling, especially on platforms like Webtoon and Tapas. "Murim" refers to a hidden martial world coexisting with ordinary society. In this realm, warriors, known as Murim-in, dedicate their lives to mastering martial arts and cultivating ki (internal energy) to achieve superhuman abilities. The Murim world is structured around various sects, clans, and factions, each with its own philosophies, techniques, and hierarchies.
