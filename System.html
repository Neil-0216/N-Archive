System

Description

The system genre in manhwa refers to stories that incorporate game-like mechanics into the characters’ reality—creating a world where levels, stats, skills, quests, and item drops influence daily life. This genre blends elements of fantasy, action, and RPG-style progression, offering readers an engaging experience that feels like watching a living video game unfold.
